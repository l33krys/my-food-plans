Baked Spaghetti

Ingredients

Instructions

Meat sauce

  1. In a pan, saute onions for about 5 min
  2. Add in meat and cook
  3. Drain fat
  4. Add in garlic, herbs, worchestire
  5. Add ragu sauce and heat thoroughly

Spaghetti

  1. Boil spaghetti per package directions
  2. Toss spaghetti in melted butter, egg, parmesan, garlic powder

Assembly

  1. Layer half of the spaghetti in a 9x9 baking pan
  2. Spread half ricotta, meat sauce, mozzarella on noodles
  3. Repeat
  4. Cover with foil and bake at 350 degrees for 30 min
  5. Remove foil and bake for another 10-15 min until golden brown or broil for quicker time
  6. Let it cool for 5 min before serving
